Engagement Models

Engagement is a step towards collaborative success. The goal of Engagement models is to best align an execution strategy based on corresponding technology and client’s business needs. We offer flexible engagement models those are designed to fulfill varied requirements and establish lifelong relationships with customers. These are the proven models industry wide to provide operational efficiency and quality at reduced cost.

Hybrid Model

Hybrid model as the name suggest is a “Hybrid” approach which provides local project supervision and offshore development. Clients can have the benefit of local project management and interaction by having a project manager in their own country and leverage cost effective development by having offshore development from India.


More

  • Communication overlapping Time Zone
  • Zero Communication Gap
  • Greater Operational Benefits
  • Increased Quality Control

Fixed Cost Model

Fixed cost model is when project requirements and specifications are well defined based on which a fixed time and cost estimation is provided. Once the project starts entire team of Project Manager, Designers, Developers, Database Experts, Quality Assurance Engineers are assigned for the project execution. This model is well fitted for small-size projects.


More

  • Well defined Scope of Work by Tech Experts
  • Complete control over costing
  • Accommodate changing requirements easily
  • Agile Implementation with well-defined Sprints

Offshore Development Center

Offshore Development Centers (ODCs) are comprised of highly skilled Technical experts from Techneeqs in collaboration with your team. Our in-house team functions as extended part of your own team. We assist corporations’ set-up and operate their team in India and provide turnkey solution for the same.


More

  • Hire a skilled resource to meet deadlines
  • Communication in overlapping Time Zone
  • Cost Effective Offshore Development
  • Flexibility to Scale Team

Time and Material Model

Time and Material model is basically when you hire skilled experts – Designers, Developers, Database Experts or Quality Analysts on hourly, monthly, or enduring contracts. It is very cost effective and agile model mostly suited for midsize and enterprise projects.


More

  • Flexibility to scale up or down team as per business needs
  • Work can be done on single or multiple projects
  • Detailed transparency and control over project progress
  • Stay up to date with progress of project and deliverables
